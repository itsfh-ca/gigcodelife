Running two apps at once sounds like free money — twice the offers, less dead time. The trap is that both apps assume they own you the moment you accept, and accepting a second order while mid-first is how completion rates quietly collapse.

The rule that keeps it clean: **only one app holds a live acceptance at a time.** Keep both apps *listening*, but the instant you accept on one, the other goes to "not accepting" in your head until that order is dropped. You still see every offer. You just never owe two clocks at once.

Do it the sloppy way and you'll cancel to save the better order, and the cancel — not the decline — is the number that follows you around.
