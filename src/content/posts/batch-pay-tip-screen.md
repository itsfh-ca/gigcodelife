Stacked orders are where the pay screen gets slippery. The app shows one combined number for two drops, and that single figure quietly folds a known tip on order A into an unknown base on order B. You accept the batch, complete both, and only in the receipt do you see the split.

The tell is the delivery you didn't want. If a batch pays well, it's usually one generous tipper subsidizing one no-tipper the app couldn't move on its own. You're not being offered two good orders. You're being offered one good order with a tax.

**What I do now:** treat the combined number as the *minimum*, not the deal. If it doesn't clear my per-drop floor on its own, the hidden order has to be nearly free for me to say yes — because that's what the app is betting it is.
